Mappings of Web data vocabularies to Schema.org terms ...

DBpedia

The DBpedia ontology is a collection of terms from the DBpedia project. The mapping from DBpedia to Schema.org terms is available.

Dublin Core

Dublin Core is a general purpose vocabulary. The mapping will be available soon ...

FOAF

FOAF is a vocabulary for describing people, their characteristics and social networking features. The mapping will be available soon ...

GoodRelations

GoodRelations is a vocabulary to describe products and product offerings. The mapping will be available soon ...

SIOC

SIOC is a vocabulary for describing online communities and posts (blogs, mailing lists, Wiki pages, etc.). The mapping from SIOC to Schema.org terms is available.
